The Reshoring Advantage

A Company Can Bring Any Existing Part.
Within Minutes, It's American Again.

With Theis Tekton, a company brings any existing part or prototype to the Theis facility. Theis sprays a production-grade mold from that part — at a fraction of overseas cost, with no months-long lead time, and no compromise on precision. What was previously an insurmountable barrier to reshoring becomes a single-day event.
