Target production rate: one 40-foot pole every five minutes, domestic production, zero rot, zero termite susceptibility, fire-resistant. No more trees need be cut down for a telephone pole — ever again.

XTT
Containment

Theis Manufacturing Corp's response to one of the fastest-growing and most dangerous gaps in American safety infrastructure: the containment of lithium batteries, hazardous materials, explosives, and radiological materials across all modes of transport and storage. Li-battery fires caused $2.5B in U.S. recycling and storage facility damage in 2025. 5,000+ MRF fires occur annually. No certified engineered containment standard yet exists for the next generation of threats. XTT is designed to create and own that standard.

4×4×4 Forkliftable Li-Battery Overpack
Engineered to contain thermal runaway, blast, toxic fumes, and fire — forkliftable, stackable, DOT/PHMSA/FAA pathway active. Testing in progress. The only engineered solution designed for the new generation of Li-battery storage threats.
Schedule 40 Replacement — Energetics

Modular Containment Vaults

XTT Full Suite — All Transport Modes
