Clean Chemistry

Environmental
Profile

Theis Tekton eliminates the toxic legacy chemistries that have defined industrial coatings for decades — without compromising performance.

100%

Zero VOC Solids

No solvent carrier — nothing evaporates, nothing off-gasses, nothing left behind in the environment. 100% solids formulation.

0

PFAS Compounds

Designed without persistent fluorinated compounds that have become a regulatory and public health liability globally. PFAS-free formulation.

Production Waste

Every manufacturing residual converts into usable building material — not landfill. Does not support bacterial growth. Non-leaching, non-degrading.
