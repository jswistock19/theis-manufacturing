Diligence

Risk Controls &
Diligence Path

Technical Claims

Performance characteristics are engineered targets subject to testing, certification, and jurisdictional approval. The company maintains a live evidence room under NDA: ASTM reports, lab data, photos, case studies, demo videos, third-party testing, LOIs, patent materials, prototype records, and supplier capacity documentation.

IP Protection

Trade secrets, process know-how, patents and patent filings, prototype records, and formulation library held under LP Miller LLC. Compartmentalized access controls. No claim represented as certified unless an independent third-party report or regulatory clearance is on file and cited.

Market Risk Mitigation

Early-revenue product sequencing decouples near-term cash flow from regulated market timelines. Multiple independent divisions prevent single-product concentration. Government program pursuit provides non-dilutive acceleration capital and validation credibility.

Team & Execution Risk

Above-market compensation, positive culture, BlackBox AI reducing friction — these are structural retention strategies, not promises. Theis Construction grew to 1,000+ direct employees and 1,000+ active subcontractors under Jason Theis leadership — demonstrating operational scale at this level.

Supply Chain

>1M gallons per month raw material access with no known single-source bottleneck. U.S.-Controlled Supply Chain Audit for every priority product line. BlackBox Manufacturing AI monitors adversarial supply-chain exposure and flags sourcing risk proactively.

Regulatory Sequencing

Every division is sequenced by readiness. Products requiring only commercial market entry launch first. Federally regulated products advance through required pathways in parallel. No division is dependent on another's certification for early revenue.
