Compliance

Standards We
Meet

Theis Manufacturing pursues and maintains compliance with the most rigorous regulatory standards in America — ensuring every system we deliver is code-compliant, certification-ready, and built for long-term performance in regulated environments.

Federal Compliance
DOT Compliance

Department of Transportation standards for containment and transport composite systems. XTT Containment Division is currently in active compliance testing for DOT-regulated applications involving hazardous material transport vessels.

Pipeline Safety
PHMSA

Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration compliance for hydrocarbon containment applications. Theis systems for pipeline and hazardous material containment are engineered to PHMSA standards from the ground up.

Aviation Standards
FAA

Federal Aviation Administration compliance testing underway for aviation-adjacent composite systems. XTT Containment and relevant Theis material systems are advancing through FAA review for airside and aviation facility applications.

Defense Compliance
ITAR

International Traffic in Arms Regulations compliance for Alviz Defense production and export. All defense-related composite systems produced by Theis Manufacturing are managed under ITAR protocols to ensure full regulatory compliance for military and defense applications.

Engineering Standards
ASME

American Society of Mechanical Engineers standards for Theis Hyperbaric chamber construction. All hyperbaric vessel designs are engineered to ASME pressure vessel code requirements, ensuring safety and regulatory acceptance for medical and wellness applications.

Medical Device
FDA 510k

FDA medical device pathway in progress for Theis Hyperbaric commercial units. The 510k premarket notification pathway is being pursued for composite hyperbaric chambers, enabling deployment in clinical, wellness, and military medical environments across the United States.

Three Deployment
Models
