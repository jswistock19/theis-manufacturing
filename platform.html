What Is Theis Tekton

A Complete
Composite System

Theis Tekton is not a single product — it is a full system of over 1,100 proprietary polymer composite formulations engineered under one platform. Every formulation is designed for a specific outcome, whether that is structural reinforcement, fire resistance, ballistic protection, chemical containment, or environmental sealing. The breadth of the library is what makes Theis capable of serving twelve distinct industrial verticals from a single manufacturing foundation.

The workhorse formulation is TT7200 — a high-adhesion structural composite with a 3–10 second cure time and military-grade durability that bonds to concrete, steel, wood, and FRP. P11FRC is the primer system that bonds to virtually any substrate surface, providing the adhesion foundation that the rest of the BLT stack builds upon. Together, these two formulations underpin the majority of Theis applications across all divisions.

Every formulation is licensed through HP Miller LLC and manufactured in the USA. The platform deploys across 12 industry verticals with division-specific variants for ballistic, marine, chemical, and environmental applications — all sharing the same core material science, manufacturing logic, and quality system.

Application System

The BLT
Stack

Every Theis application follows the same five-layer BLT sandwich principle — ensuring maximum adhesion, durability, and performance.

LAYER 5
Topcoat / Protective Finish

UV, chemical, and abrasion resistance. Final barrier.

LAYER 4
Functional Layer

Division-specific: ballistic, marine, chemical, thermal.

LAYER 3
Structural Core — TT7200

Primary strength layer. 3–10 second cure. Military-grade.

LAYER 2
Primer — P11FRC

Maximum adhesion to any substrate surface.

LAYER 1
Substrate Preparation

Surface profiling and contamination removal.

Every layer is a Theis Tekton formulation. Every assembly is custom-engineered.

How Each Layer Works

L5

Topcoat — The Outer Shield

The final coat blocks UV degradation, chemical ingress, and mechanical abrasion. Color-stable and long-life. Customized per division and environment.

L4

Functional Layer — Division-Specific

This layer carries the specialized performance for each application: ballistic rated for Alviz Defense, salt-resistant for marine, heat-tolerant for energy, or fire-resistant for construction.

L3

TT7200 Structural Core — The Workhorse

The primary strength layer. TT7200 cures in 3–10 seconds, delivers military-grade structural integrity, and bonds permanently to virtually any substrate. Used in 8 of 12 divisions.

L2

P11FRC Primer — The Bond Guarantee

High-adhesion primer that creates a chemically compatible interface with virtually any substrate. No P11FRC — no guarantee. This layer is not optional.

L1

Substrate Preparation — The Foundation

Surface profiling and contamination removal. Clean, profiled substrate is the prerequisite for every application. Theis crews handle this on-site before any material touches the surface.

How We Deploy

Three Ways to
Work With Us

Theis Tekton reaches your project through whichever deployment path fits your application, scale, and timeline.

01
Direct Application

Theis field crews apply composite systems directly on-site. Ideal for infrastructure, construction, and large-scale industrial projects where precision application and quality control are non-negotiable. Our crews own the process end to end — substrate prep through topcoat.

02
Licensed Applicators via Parresia Corp

Certified third-party applicators trained and licensed by Theis through Parresia Corp. Extends national geographic reach without compromising material quality or application standards. Every licensed applicator is trained to Theis spec — not a third-party standard.

03
Manufactured Product

Pre-fabricated composite components produced in the Theis facility. Ready to install upon delivery. Used for hyperbaric chambers, containment liners, specialty panels, and custom-engineered components where field application is not optimal.
