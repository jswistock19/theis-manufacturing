What We Make

One Material.
Twelve Industries.

The Theis Tekton polymer composite system is a proprietary, field-proven material platform built around a five-layer BLT (Bonded Layer Technology) architecture. Every application — from structural coatings to armor to containment — flows from the same engineered foundation.

The BLT system moves from substrate preparation through primer bond layer, structural core, functional performance layer, and protective topcoat — creating a composite system that outperforms traditional materials in tensile strength, chemical resistance, impact tolerance, and service life.
